# Positioning options #

**Top location**
> `ControlStage.TOP`

**Bottom location**
> `ControlStage.BOTTOM`

**Left location**
> `ControlStage.LEFT`

**Right location**
> `ControlStage.RIGHT`

**Centered location**
> `ControlStage.CENTER`

## Combinations ##

You can combine options, for example:

> `ControlStage.TOP` + `ControlStage.RIGHT`
